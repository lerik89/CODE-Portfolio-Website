Scribble — a minimalistic note-taking app

Scribble is a note-taking app for those moments, when you just wish you had a pen at hand.

It’s classic look and style mirrors the iconic yellow pad, but with a contemporary twist for the digital age.

Easy to navigate, quick to edit, no-frills, yet with all essential features of a modern word-processor in place.

Forget pen and paper, take out your phone!

Scribble was a short project in the context of my studies of UI Design with Careerfoundry.

It was completed within a few days in January 2021. The objective was to quickly design a few screens to present to a potential client that is looking for a less-is-more, simple and intuitive note-taking app.

My aim was to design a clearly structured, no frills, simple and elegant app, that features all essentials of a modern word-processor, yet is unique through it’s recognizable and nostalgic visual style reminiscent of the iconic yellow pad.
