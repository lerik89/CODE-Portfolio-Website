Waste-Less — an ingredients-based recipe app

Waste-Less is a web-based cooking app that runs a smart search algorithm allowing users to look up recipes with the ingredients they already have in their pantry.

Additionally, users also can curate a shopping list for missing ingredients by simply clicking “add missing to list” on the recipe page. This makes Waste-Less functionality unique and helps the user save money, time and develop a better meal-planning routine.
